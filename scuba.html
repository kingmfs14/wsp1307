Scuba Excursions

We Offer the following:

  • One tank excursion
  • Two tank excursion
  • Three tank excursion

Scuba diving is an exhilarating adventure for advance swimmers looking to see the beauty in the depths of the sea. We offer weekly classes with highly qualified ocean specialist. On these adventures, you get up close and personal with the fish, crabs, dolphins, turtles, and sharks. We will provide all the gear necessary including wet suits, air tanks, masks, and flippers. Throughout the week of training, we will explore hundreds of snorkeling sights. Each day going deeper than the previous day.

Our daily one tank adventure is a great place for newly certified divers to begin. It will give you a taste for the true ocean and the sea life you have never seen before.

We offer two different two tank excursions. We offer a two day, one tank excursion each day. This option gives you a wider options for diving for the curious diver. With two completely different locations and the option to pick the location depending on availability, it is no wonder that this is our most popular option. We also offer a two tank single day excursion. This option we will take you to a single location, then have lunch onboard the boat. Then hop back in a spot nearby.

Our three day excursion is a full day excursion. We start by going out first thing in the morning. While on our way to our second location, we will have lunch onboard the boat. Following our lunch, we will jump back in off the coast of the island of Lanai. After this dive, we will dock at the harbor to allow you to enjoy a dinner there. Once we are all back on the ship, we will go to a popular night dive spot off of lahaina.
