Site Intent

The website deign objective for Maui Underwater Excursions is to create a simple underwater themed site. The site will have seven individual pages associated with the website. One page for each excursion offered by the client, home, and contact page.

By having separate pages for each excursion will give the visitor individual attention to each excursion which will boost sales. By incorporating the social media into the contact page, marketing should increase by word of mouth and social streams.

Targeted Audience

The targeted audience is to include both tourist visited the island from the mainland or another country and locals from either maui or another neighboring island. The tourist audience is the primary focus of the site since a great deal of the income for this business is through the tourism to Maui. The tourist need to have a clear and concise information to avoid confusion. This audience is very tech savvy knowing the ins and outs of websites.

The overall feel of the site should portray an island feel which should attract our local audience. This audience knows their general way around the island and need more of a focus on the excursions that are offered. They are not as tech savvy, but have the technology which will require quick loading time.
